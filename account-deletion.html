Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

We respect your right to data privacy and provide two methods for requesting account deletion from Outventure:

Method 1: Through the App

You can delete your account directly through the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Outventure app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Select Settings
  4. Navigate to Account
  5. Click "Delete Account"

Method 2: Via Email

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by sending an email to wilkeleon2@gmail.com from your registered account email address.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information (email, user ID)
  • Challenge history and completion records
  • Personal reflections and journal entries
  • Profile pictures
  • Feedback submissions
  • Usage statistics and activity data

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 48 hours. Once completed, all associated data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at wilkeleon2@gmail.com.
